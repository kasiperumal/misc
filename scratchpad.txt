Page Views per Session: The average number of pages viewed by the user during a single session.
Average Time on Page: The average amount of time the user spends on a single page.
Click-through Rate (CTR): The ratio of clicks on specific elements (e.g., buttons, links) to total views.
Bounce Rate: The percentage of sessions where the user only views one page before leaving the site.
Navigation Path: Describes the common paths or sequences of pages visited by the user.
Device Usage: Indicates the types of devices used by the user (e.g., desktop, mobile, tablet).
Engagement Metrics: A composite measure of user interaction levels, including frequency of interactions, use of advanced features, and regularity of actions like transactions.
